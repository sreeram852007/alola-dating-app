To generate proper icons, visit this site on your phone:
https://tools.picsart.com/image/icon-generator/

Or use this simple method:
1. Go to https://www.icoconverter.com/
2. Upload any heart image
3. Generate sizes: 72,96,128,144,152,192,384,512
4. Download and rename to icon-[size]x[size].png
5. Place them in this folder (client/public/)

For quick testing, Chrome will auto-generate icons from the manifest.
